What is collision coverage in auto insurance?

Collision coverage helps pay for repairs or replacement of your car if it's damaged in an accident, regardless of who’s at fault.

Is it mandatory to have auto insurance in Lebanon Virginia ?

Yes, the state of Lebanon Virginia requires all drivers to carry a minimum level of liability auto insurance coverage.
