What is the difference between liability and full coverage auto insurance?

Liability insurance covers damage you cause to others, while full coverage includes both liability and coverage for your own vehicle's damages.

What is collision coverage in auto insurance?

Collision coverage helps pay for repairs or replacement of your car if it's damaged in an accident, regardless of who’s at fault.
