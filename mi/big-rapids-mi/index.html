In Big Rapids Michigan auto insurance is required by law, but that doesn’t mean all policies are the same. The key factors to consider include liability coverage, comprehensive and collision insurance, and personal injury protection. Liability coverage helps protect you financially in case you’re at fault for an accident, covering the other party’s damages.
