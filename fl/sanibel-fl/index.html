In Sanibel Florida auto insurance is required by law, but that doesn’t mean all policies are the same. The key factors to consider include liability coverage, comprehensive and collision insurance, and personal injury protection. Liability coverage helps protect you financially in case you’re at fault for an accident, covering the other party’s damages. Comprehensive insurance covers damages from incidents like vandalism, natural disasters, and theft. Collision coverage, on the other hand, helps repair your vehicle after an accident, regardless of fault.
